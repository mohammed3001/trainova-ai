# Trainova AI — Investor Pitch (Concise)

> **The Global Marketplace for AI Training Talent.**
> Hire. Test. Trust. Train.

---

## 1. The Opportunity
Every company building with AI needs trained, tested experts — for fine-tuning, prompt engineering, RLHF, safety alignment, evaluation, agents, data labeling and domain tuning. Today that talent is scattered across LinkedIn, Upwork, private Slack groups, and expensive consultancies. **There is no trusted, vertical, evaluation-first marketplace for this talent.**

## 2. The Product
**Trainova AI** is a single platform that unifies three products:

1. **Marketplace** — companies post highly customizable AI training requests; specialists apply.
2. **Evaluation Engine** — companies link an API / sandbox / evaluation environment; candidates are tested *on the actual model or a representative environment* with automated + human scoring.
3. **Collaboration Workspace** — advanced chat, AI summaries, contracts, e-signature, escrow, milestones, payouts.

All managed through a **no-code admin mission control** built for non-technical operators.

## 3. What Makes Us Different
- **Model-Linked Testing** — the only platform that tests candidates on the client's actual AI environment.
- **Vertical Focus** — purpose-built for AI Training roles, not generalist freelance.
- **Trust Layer** — verification, badges, escrow, audit-ready records.
- **AI-Native** — internal AI for matching, profile optimization, SEO, chat summaries, test generation.
- **Global-First** — multilingual, RTL/LTR, regional compliance built-in.

## 4. Business Model (Five Revenue Streams)
1. **Subscriptions** — Companies (Free / Pro / Enterprise), Trainers (Basic / Verified Pro / Premium).
2. **Take Rate** — 5%–15% commission on completed contracts.
3. **Premium Services** — verification, managed hiring, AI Request Builder, AI Candidate Screener.
4. **Advertising** — sponsored listings, category sponsorship, newsletter, homepage, sidebar; targeted at AI infrastructure vendors (API providers, Cloud, MLOps, labeling tools).
5. **Enterprise** — white-label portal, API access, bespoke integrations, SLAs.

## 5. Go-to-Market
- **Supply-first**: onboard 500+ verified trainers through direct outreach, AI community partnerships, and badge programs.
- **SEO Engine**: programmatic pages for every skill / specialization / country generate compounding inbound traffic from day one.
- **Demand**: outbound to AI-first startups, LLM infra companies, and enterprise AI teams; partnerships with Cloud & API providers for co-marketing.

## 6. Roadmap
- **0–3 months**: MVP (accounts, profiles, requests, applications, basic tests, chat, admin, subscriptions, i18n, SEO).
- **3–6 months**: Model-linked live testing, escrow payments, verification, full ads platform.
- **6–12 months**: AI Matching Engine, enterprise dashboards, white-label, video interviews, fraud detection.

## 7. KPIs We Track
MAU (companies / trainers), Verified Trainers, GMV, Take Rate, Application→Hire conversion, CAC/LTV, NRR (Enterprise), Ads eCPM.

## 8. The Ask
Seed round to:
- Ship production MVP and onboard first 1,000 trainers / 100 companies.
- Build the Evaluation Engine and AI Matching.
- Hire GTM + Trust & Safety.

**Trainova AI — the trust layer for hiring AI training talent, globally.**
